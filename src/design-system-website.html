What makes a great Design System Website?

Detailed Documentation, concise release notes, changelogs, consistency, ease of use and open source frame of mind.

For the library to succeed we ensured developers had a great experience. To accomplish this, we determined what made a successful library by conducting several developer interviews and competitive analysis.
